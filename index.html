Volatility Modeling in Crypto Futures

Using the Heston model to predict volatility regimes on Bybit.

1. The Math

We model the stochastic volatility using the standard Heston SDE:

$$ dS_t = \mu S_t dt + \sqrt{v_t} S_t dW_t^S $$ $$ dv_t = \kappa (\theta - v_t) dt + \sigma \sqrt{v_t} dW_t^v $$
